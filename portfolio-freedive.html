What is a content management system or CMS?

A CMS is an application that is connected to your website, that allows you to edit and make changes to the website without having any knowledge of coding. Allowing you or your staff to update and edit your website easily. Examples of content management systems are Wordpress, Joomla, Wix and Squarespace. However not all CMS systems are created equal and some are easier to use then others.
